France

By Jack Mac Namara

France, also known as République Francaise, is the oldest and largest country in the EU and one of the most important in the world. Its exquisite cuisines, magnificent art, culture and captivating history and landscapes, make it a country that is truly spectacular.

Iceland

By Scott Brady

Discover the volcanoes, geysers, lava fields, mountains and glaciers of the Europe's second-largest island, commonly known as “The Land of Fire and Ice” and its long summer days with nearly 24 hours of sunshine, offset by shorter winter days with only a few hours of light.

Nigeria

By Okikiola Sanni

Nigeria is a diversified country in the West of Africa. It is known to be the “Giant of Africa”. It is a home to millions of individuals, and over 250 ethnic groups, more than 500 languages and butterfly species.

New Zealand

By Peadar McHenry

New Zealand is an island country located in the south-western Pacific Ocean. It consists of two islands. It is a country filled with rich culture which mainly comes from the ethnic group called Māori.

Spain

By Connor Byrne

Spain is a country located in south western Europe that is home to the second most spoken language in the world, Spanish. Spain is famous for its good food, great people, its scenic beauty and its vast coastline.
