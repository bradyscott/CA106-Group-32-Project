Spain has some of the nicest food in the world, here are some of their dishes:

Tapas:

Tapas aren’t just a certain type of food; they are an entire subsection of food. They come in hundreds of different variations and consist of many different foods. In Spain they are used as a simple snack while you meet up with your amigos and drink a beverage of your choice, such as beer or wine.

Tortilla Española (Spanish Omelette)

One of Spain’s most iconic dishes, it consists of eggs, potatoes and onions. The potatoes and onions are slow fried in olive oil then mixed with the beaten eggs for the flavours to mix before cooking.

Paella

This is one of Spain’s most famous dishes. It is originally from Valencia, first made in the mid-19th century. It is made from rice, chicken or rabbit and a mix of vegetables.

Calamari

This is a delicious appetizer that is made with squid and then battered and deep-fried for an irresistible crispy taste. It is known to have many health benefits such as being rich in protein and helps prevent cancer.

Gazpacho

This dish originates from mainland Spain's most southerly region, Andalucia. This soup contains cucumber, garlic, onion, pepper, and tomato with olive oil, salt, water and wine vinegar.
