Spanish is the official language of Spain. It is a Romance language that originated in the Iberian Peninsula of Europe; today, it is a global language with nearly 500 million native speakers, mainly in Spain and the Americas. It is the world's second-most spoken native language, after Mandarin Chinese.

Here are some common Spanish phrases:

Below, there are a few basic spanish words/phrases. Try listening to the audio.

  1. Hola

    2. Pronounced: oh-lah

    This means 'Hello', it is used the same as you would in English.

  2. Sí

    3. Pronounced: Sea

    This means 'yes'.

  3. No

    4. Pronounced: no

    This means 'no'.

  4. Buenos días

    5. Pronounced: bweh-nohs dee-ahs

    This means 'good day' and it is probably the most common greeting in the Spanish language.

  5. Gracias

    6. Pronounced: grah-syahs

    This means 'Thank you'.

  6. ¿Cuánto cuesta?

    7. Pronounced: kwahn-toh kwehs-tah

    This means 'How much?'. You could use this when buying something in a shop.

  7. De Nada

    8. Pronounced: deh nah-dah

    This means 'You're welcome'.

  8. No entiendo

    9. Pronounced: noh ehn-tyehn-doh

    This means 'I don't understand'.

  9. Me llamo

    10. Pronounced: meh yah-moh

    This means 'My name is...'.

  10. ¿Dónde está el baño?

    11. Pronounced: dohn-deh ehs-tah el bah-nyoh)

    This basically means 'Where is the bathroom?'.
