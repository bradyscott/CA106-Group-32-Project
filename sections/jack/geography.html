Location

France by in large is the largest country in western Europe with a land mass of approx. 643,801 km². France’s Capital City Paris accounts for nearly 11 million habitants of the approx. 68 million habitants across the land. France shares eight borders with neighbouring counties, all of which can be seen from the below map:

France’s natural landmarks are quite simply picturesque. The Loire (which source is in Mont Gerbier de Jonc) is the longest river in France and flows for an astonishing 1,006 km and is the worlds 171st largest river. The Loire drains 20% of its land area as well.

Not only is France home to the Loire, however it is also home to the Pyrenees mountains with is the largest mountain range in France (which stretches 270 km long ) and is located in the south of France and forms a natural boarder between Spain.

As we have learned from the previous paragraphs, we now know that the Pyrenees Mountain is the largest mountain range in France, although what is the largest mountain?? Mount Blonc is an astonishing 4,810m high and is the highest mountain in France and creates a border between Italy and France.
