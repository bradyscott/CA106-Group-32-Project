French is the most common langauge spkoen in France followed by English and the French Langauge is the second most popular language in Europe. Also, a know fact, the French Language is one of the most significant romance languages in the world.

What makes French a complicated language to undertand is that there is many different virations of the language all across the country. 'Parisin French' came to be the ideal picture for the French langauge due to its important part in French Culture and history.

Even with the different viarations of the language that exist, the over structure of the language has stayed ther same for the most part. However the way in which the French pronounce their language (depending on which provience you are in) can be proncued differently to other cities and region.
